Copywriting

Creative Copywriting?

Creative copywriting is the technique of writing interesting, persuasive, and unique text or content with the aim of promoting an organization, activity, and competition.

Within marketing?

In the context of marketing, creative copywriting serves to attract the attention of the audience, convey a clear message, and motivate them to sign up for an ongoing service or activity.

Essentials

  1. Creativity
  2. Clarity
  3. Persuasive

Story Board

Storyboards are visual representations of ideas or concepts that will be realized in the form of media, such as advertisements, videos, or presentations.

Elements of Storyboard

  1. Picture or Sketch
  2. Text Description
  3. Sequence
  4. Dialogue or Narration

Marketing Plan

Marketing plan functions to compile what is needed for a work program, the output of this marketing plan includes: Feeds, IG Story Templates, Google Form Headers and Cover Reels.

How to make engaging content

  1. The image or visual displayed must be interesting, if you need a source you can search on pinterest, etc.
  2. Find interesting ideas and follow trends, to explore ideas can be through existing social media such as tiktok, instagram, etc.
  3. Make sure to create an interesting and persuasive title to attract people to read the content

Creating Eye-catching Writing

  1. Use emoticons to clarify the content and make the caption more interesting to read
  2. Don't forget to use hashtags
