Photography/Videography

Camera

Parts
  • Lens
    • Focal length (smaller focal length = wider coverage and vice versa)
    • Exposure triangle :
      • Apeture (f/n), smaller n = larger lens opening
      • Shutter speed - (1/n s), ex: 1/500 → 1/500s. Larger n = faster shutter timeframe, less light received (Freeze effect obtained on high n, and vice versa)
      • ISO = sens of lens to light (lower = darker), set ISO last
  • Diaphragm
  • Fokus

Tips

  1. Fully open aperture → depth of field effect
  2. Focal length = shutter speed (minimal 1/60)
  3. ISO if aperture / shutter is set to minimalize grain
