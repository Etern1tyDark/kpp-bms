About Us.

This project is the culmination of our learning journey in the Basic Media Schooling (BMS), organized by HMIT ITS. We are Group 16, a team of four members brought together by a shared passion for media and creativity. Our logo, marked by the number 16, represents this unique group identity.

Throughout this program, we've explored the fundamentals of design, website creation, photography, videography, and copywriting. Each section of our project showcases the skills we've developed, giving you a glimpse into our creative process and growth.

We invite you to explore our works and learn more about what we've accomplished. This is just the beginning of our journey into the world of media.
